About TCC


Where Every Cup Tells a Story

Three Cups Cafe is a cozy haven nestled in the heart of the city, founded by three passionate coffee aficionados. Our cafe exudes warmth and comfort, inviting guests to unwind and savor the rich aromas of freshly brewed coffee. With a menu crafted to delight every palate, from robust espressos to creamy lattes, and a selection of artisanal pastries and snacks, Three Cups Cafe promises a delightful experience with every visit. Whether you're catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or simply enjoying a moment of tranquility, our cafe is the perfect place to indulge in the simple pleasures of life, one cup at a time.

Meet The Owners


When did it all start?
It started as a joke between the three friends, owning a business. It was Shaira's idea, to make a coffee shop, and April decided, "why not make it into reality?". Months of planning and preparation went through the business, Shaira being in-charge of the menu, April for the location, and Eduardo for the finances. The planning and preparation came into fruition on May 9, 2024, the Three Cups Cafe officially opened. Days turned to weeks, and weeks turned to months, and finally months turned to a year. With hardwork, dedication, and teamwork of the three owners, Three Cups Cafe is still up and running for now a year.
